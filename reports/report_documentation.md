# Report Outputs

This folder documents the standard reports generated by the Fixed Asset Management System.

---

## Monthly Reports

### 1. Fixed Asset Register Report
**Frequency:** Monthly
**Audience:** Senior Fixed Asset Analysts, Finance Leadership
**Contents:** Full listing of all active assets by location and category, including purchase cost, net book value, status, and last audit date
**Format:** Excel export / Access report

---

### 2. Monthly Depreciation Expense Summary
**Frequency:** Monthly
**Audience:** Finance Team, Senior Analysts
**Contents:** Total depreciation expense by location and category for the current month and year-to-date
**Format:** Excel export

---

### 3. Inventory Reconciliation Report
**Frequency:** After each physical audit cycle
**Audience:** Senior Fixed Asset Analysts, Internal Audit
**Contents:** Comparison of physical audit counts vs register records by location, with discrepancy flags and resolution status
**Format:** Excel export / Access report

---

## Quarterly Reports

### 4. Quarterly Net Book Value Summary
**Frequency:** Quarterly
**Audience:** Finance Leadership
**Contents:** Net book value by location and category at quarter end, with quarter-over-quarter change tracking
**Format:** Excel PivotTable with bar chart

---

### 5. End-of-Life Asset Report
**Frequency:** Quarterly
**Audience:** Capital Expenditure Planning Team
**Contents:** Assets within 2 years of full depreciation, flagged for replacement planning with estimated retirement dates
**Format:** Excel export

---

## Annual Reports

### 6. Annual Depreciation Schedule
**Frequency:** Annual
**Audience:** Finance Team, External Auditors
**Contents:** Complete year-by-year depreciation history per asset, including accumulated depreciation and book value at each year end
**Format:** Excel workbook (one tab per location)

---

### 7. Tangible Tax Return Support Package
**Frequency:** Annual
**Audience:** Tax/Compliance Team, County Tax Authorities
**Contents:** Asset values by location for tangible personal property tax filing, including total original cost, accumulated depreciation, and net book value per jurisdiction
**Format:** Excel export formatted for filing submission

---

## Key Metrics Tracked

| Metric | Description | Reporting Frequency |
|---|---|---|
| Total Asset Count | Number of active assets by location | Monthly |
| Total Purchase Cost | Gross asset value by location | Monthly |
| Net Book Value | Total book value after depreciation | Monthly |
| Annual Depreciation Expense | Current year depreciation by location/category | Monthly |
| Reconciliation Rate | % of assets verified without discrepancy | Per Audit Cycle |
| End-of-Life Assets | Count of assets within 2 years of retirement | Quarterly |
| Fully Depreciated Assets Still in Service | Assets at or below salvage value | Quarterly |
